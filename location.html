Conference place

The conference will take place in the building of Adam Mickiewicz University Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science (polish: Wydział Matematyki i Informatyki Uniwersytetu im. Adama Mickiewicza) in Poznań, Poland.

The exact address is:
Adam Mickiewicz University
Collegium Mathematicum
Umultowska 87 street
61-614 Poznan
phone no. 0048-791888072
fax 0048-618295315 (make RuPy conference 2008 adnotation)

How to get to the conference place

On the map you can find marked the most important places of RuPy 2008.

To get to the conference place just catch a tram 12, 14, 15 or 16 from Most Dworcowy (heading os. Sobieskiego, aprox. 15 minutes) and then walk to conference place (aprox. 10 minutes) or catch bus line 98 from Szymanowskiego (heading os. Różany Potok). Collegium Mathematicum is at bus stop Campus Morasko.
