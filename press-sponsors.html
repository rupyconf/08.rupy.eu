Webankieta

Webankieta is one of the first polish web systems built on Ruby On Rails. Today this is the leading online survey system serving more than 25,000 questionnaires prepared by users. The system can handle all the phases of survey projects. Starting from creating questionnaires through the response collection finally helping with data analysis and reporting. All that done in very user-friendly manner – unlike spreadsheets, databases and general purpose statistics packages. Webankieta’s top priority is a combination of ease of use (for new users) with the power of analysis (required by professionals).
